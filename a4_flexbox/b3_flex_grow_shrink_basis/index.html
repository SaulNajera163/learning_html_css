What is Flex-grow?

It's just the form in how you gonna distribute the space per element, in this case we have 7 pieces, the first with 1, second with 2 and the three with 4, and your gonna take the container, cut in 7 pieces and distribute based on the flex-grow.
Works best when you do not set a fixed width on the flex items. If you set width or max-width, you limit how much the items can grow. Note: If you have a specific width with px, this not gonna be applied, just for min/max and auto.
